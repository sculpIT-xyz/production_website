sculpIT.xyz is about building, investing, partnering, and creating wellness and health sciences solutioms. Our model is to have our hands in different channels, to be empowered to build solutions using latest technologies and applied sciences. We started with sculpIT.fitness aiming at fitness and wellness professionals, with the goal to provide the tools that professionals like Personal Trainers need to facilitate their modus operandi and their access to new customers. compensation.
