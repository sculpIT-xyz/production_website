What is sculpIT?
sculpIT is mostly a collection of companies coming together to build solutions for health and fitness.

sculpIT is about building, investing, partnering, and creating health and wellness sciences solutioms. Our model is to have our hands in different channels, to be empowered to build solutions using latest technologies and applied sciences. We started with sculpIT.fitness aiming at fitness and wellness professionals, with the goal to provide the tools that professionals like Personal Trainers need to facilitate their modus operandi and their access to new customers. compensation. In addition, with this new structure we will have more flexibility to diversify vertically, with focused specialized layers of independent workload operations.
